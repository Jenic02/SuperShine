About Us

Your Car Deserves
The Best Care

At Super Shine Auto Detailing, we treat every vehicle like it's our own. Located in the heart of Seattle at Rainier Ave S, we've been providing top-quality hand car wash and detailing services to our community for over a decade.

Our team of skilled professionals uses premium products and proven techniques to restore your vehicle's showroom shine. From a quick hand wash to a full interior deep cleaning, we deliver results that speak for themselves.
